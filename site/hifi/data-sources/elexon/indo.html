-- Demand profile vs day-ahead forecast
SELECT
  i.settlement_period,
  i.initial_demand_outturn_mw AS actual_mw,
  n.national_demand_mw AS forecast_mw,
  round(i.initial_demand_outturn_mw - n.national_demand_mw, 0) AS error_mw
FROM silver.indo i
JOIN silver.ndf n USING (settlement_date, settlement_period)
WHERE i.settlement_date = '2026-05-01'
ORDER BY 1;

Caveats

Things to know.

Dataset-specific notes. Also see Elexon-wide caveats.

01
Initial estimate — may revise

INDO is the first published figure. Later settlement runs produce revised demand estimates. For a final settled demand figure, check ITSDO or the gold layer daily_brief view.

02
Excludes embedded generation

Sub-1MW solar, wind, and CHP are not metered through the BSC. They appear as reduced demand in INDO, not as generation. Do not double-count them when computing residual demand.

03
Different from transmission demand (ITSDO)

INDO is always lower than ITSDO because it nets off embedded generation. Use ITSDO when you need the full transmission system picture.
