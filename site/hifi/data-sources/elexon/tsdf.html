-- Compare TSDF transmission forecast vs ITSDO actual
SELECT
  t.settlement_period,
  t.forecast_demand_mw AS tsdf_mw,
  i.initial_transmission_system_demand_outturn_mw AS actual_mw,
  round(i.initial_transmission_system_demand_outturn_mw - t.forecast_demand_mw, 0) AS error_mw
FROM silver.tsdf t
JOIN silver.itsdo i USING (settlement_date, settlement_period)
WHERE t.settlement_date = '2026-05-01' AND t.boundary = 'Transmission';

Caveats

Things to know.

Dataset-specific notes. Also see Elexon-wide caveats.

01
Two rows per period — always filter on boundary

TSDF has National and Transmission boundary rows per period. Joining directly to single-row datasets without a boundary filter will produce duplicates. Always add WHERE boundary = 'Transmission' or 'National'.

02
Similar to NDF but at transmission level

NDF national_demand_mw and TSDF boundary=National forecast_demand_mw should be close but may differ slightly due to different modelling assumptions.
