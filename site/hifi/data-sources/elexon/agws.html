Overview

What this dataset is.

agws (AGWS / B1630) is the ENTSO-E transparency dataset for actual or estimated wind and solar generation. It covers three PSR types: Wind Onshore, Wind Offshore, and Solar — giving you the clearest picture of renewable output in any single Elexon dataset.

Critically, agws includes embedded generation estimates for solar and onshore wind — capacity that isn't metered through the Balancing Mechanism and therefore missing from fuelhh. This makes it the best source for total GB renewable output.

Data is settled D+1 and sourced from /datasets/AGWS.

-- Total wind + solar by day (incl. embedded)
SELECT
  settlement_date,
  round(sum(generation_mw) FILTER (WHERE psr_type LIKE 'Wind%') / 48, 1) AS avg_wind_mw,
  round(sum(generation_mw) FILTER (WHERE psr_type = 'Solar') / 48, 1) AS avg_solar_mw
FROM silver.agws
WHERE settlement_date >= current_date - interval '30 days'
GROUP BY 1 ORDER BY 1;

Caveats

Things to know.

Dataset-specific notes. Also see Elexon-wide caveats.

01
D+1 lag — not for real-time use

Like agpt, agws is only available the day after. For intraday solar/wind monitoring use fuelinst (note: embedded generation excluded there).

02
Solar includes embedded estimates

The Solar PSR type in agws includes behind-the-meter solar estimated by National Grid. This is not metered data — it is modelled. Treat solar figures as estimates, not measurements.

03
Onshore wind may differ from fuelhh WIND

agws Wind Onshore + Offshore should broadly match fuelhh WIND but can differ due to embedded generation inclusion and document revision timing.
